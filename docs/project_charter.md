# Project Charter
## Investment Due Diligence: Kenyan Digital Lending Sector

**Date:** February 2026  
**Analyst:** Geoffrey Mutuku  
**Status:** In Progress

## Objective
To determine whether capital deployment into a Kenyan digital consumer 
lending platform in 2026 would generate acceptable risk-adjusted returns 
under base and stressed economic scenarios.

## Decision Framework
- Base IRR must exceed 25%
- Recession IRR must remain above 10%
- Probability of capital loss must remain below 20%

## Data Sources
- Central Bank of Kenya
- Kenya National Bureau of Statistics
- World Bank Open Data
- IMF Financial Access Survey
- Safaricom Annual Reports
- Kaggle (proxy borrower dataset, calibrated to Kenya)

## Deliverables
1. Excel Investment Model (8 sheets)
2. Python Risk Model (4 notebooks)
3. Tableau Executive Dashboard
4. Investment Committee Deck (12 slides)
5. Investment Memo (6 pages)

## Disclaimer
All borrower-level modelling uses proxy datasets calibrated to Kenyan 
macro indicators. No proprietary data was used.
